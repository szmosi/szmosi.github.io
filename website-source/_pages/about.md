## Hello Digital Traveler

If you’ve found your way here, you’ve stumbled into my digital laboratory.

I am **Marton**, a Data Scientist and Business Process Developer currently based in Denmark. My work is driven by a simple philosophy: **if it’s repetitive, automate it; if it’s messy, structure it.**

I spend my time:
* **Decoding Data:** Turning thousands of scattered data points into clear, visual stories.
* **Building Bridges:** Connecting diverse data sources through APIs and automated pipelines.
* **Refining AI:** Exploring the boundaries of NLP and RAG models to make information more accessible.

### Beyond the Screen
When I am not optimizing workflows or writing Python scripts, I am likely testing a new recipe in the kitchen, deep in a strategic board game, or staying active with sports.

### The Projects
The real "magic" happens in the code. I am currently tidying up my repository and uploading my latest builds. 

To see what I’ve been working on—from BI platforms to automation tools—head over to the **[Projects](/projects)** tab.

*Hope is the last thing to die... but a well-built script is a close second.*

Best,  
**Marton**
